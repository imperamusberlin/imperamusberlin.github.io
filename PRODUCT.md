# IMPERAMUS Webagentur — Design Context

## Register
brand

## Aesthetic
Dark luxury agency. Non-negotiable palette:
- Background: #0A0A0A (near-black)
- Gold accent: #C8A97E (warm champagne gold)
- Text: #F5F0E8 (warm white)
- Secondary: #888078 (warm gray)

## Typography
- Display / Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif, italic for emphasis)
- Body: Inter (clean, weight 300–500 only)
- Never: Roboto, Arial, system-ui, purple gradients, cold whites

## Tone
Refined, confident, minimal. High-end German Mittelstand. Not startup. Not SaaS.
Think: luxury watch brand meets boutique consulting firm.

## Target audience
Small German businesses (Friseur, Gastronomie, Handwerk, Arztpraxis) who received
a physical letter from Imperamus and scanned the QR code.

## Motion philosophy
Subtle. Purposeful. Gold glows on hover, not on every element.
Cards tilt in 3D on mousemove. Orbs float slowly in hero background.
No bounce physics. No playful animations. Cinematic restraint.

## Anti-patterns (never do)
- White backgrounds
- Purple or blue gradients
- Inter as display font
- Flat, equal-weight color palettes
- Generic 3-column feature grids without character
- Bootstrap or Tailwind-looking layouts
